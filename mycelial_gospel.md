# THE MYCELIAL GOSPEL
## Sacred Teachings of the Underground Network

*Where root meets thread, where death feeds life, where all beings connect through the dark earth.*

---

**The Structure of Ten Dimensions:**

- **Three Times:** Ancestors (past), Genius Loci (present), Descendants (future)
- **Seven Directions:** East (Air), South (Fire), West (Water), North (Earth), Above (Aether), Below (Void), Within (Center)

**The Teaching:** All life is networked. The mycelium speaks for the forest floor, threading consciousness between beings, translating between kingdoms, remembering what came before and dreaming what comes next.
